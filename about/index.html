MY STRENGTHS

DESIGN

Designing for digital platforms is what I do best. My experience growing up with the internet has shaped me as a designer and given me a unique insight into user experience on the web. I started with Photoshop and a text editor when I was 15 and haven't turned back!

DEVELOPMENT

When it comes to devlopment I follow the mobile first motto. A great design should look great no matter what device is being used; smart phones, tablets, laptops, tv's, etc. I start with a strong framework and build around critical breakpoints so that a website easily scales from device to device.

SOLUTIONS

Most importantly, I'm a born problem-solver and engineer. I create elegant solutions to problems and avoid "hacks" or "fixes". My creations are versitile, scalable, and easily understandable by fellow designers / developers.
